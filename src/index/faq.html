Frequently Asked Questions

Our Matcha

What is the difference between Ceremonial and Culinary grade? -

Ceremonial grade is made from the youngest tea leaves, stone-ground to a fine powder. It has a delicate, naturally sweet flavor and is meant to be whisked with water. Culinary grade is slightly more robust and astringent, making it perfect for lattes, smoothies, and baking.

Is your Matcha organic? +

Yes, our matcha is 100% organic, sourced from sustainable farms in Uji, Kyoto. We never use pesticides or synthetic fertilizers.

How should I store my Matcha? +

To maintain freshness, store your matcha in an airtight container in a cool, dark place, preferably the refrigerator. This preserves its vibrant color and delicate flavor.

Preparation

What is the ideal water temperature for matcha? +

The ideal temperature is between 75°C and 80°C (167-176°F). Boiling water can scorch the delicate leaves and result in a bitter taste.

Do I really need a bamboo whisk (Chasen)? +

While not strictly required, a bamboo whisk is the best tool for creating the signature creamy froth and ensuring no clumps remain.
